Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Head Copy and Morse code training

What is "head copy" in Morse code?

Head copy is the ability to understand Morse code directly in your mind without writing it down. It's the gold standard for CW proficiency, allowing you to participate in conversations naturally without needing a pencil and paper.

How does Head Copy differ from other CW training apps?

Unlike apps that use random text or outdated content, Head Copy features realistic QSO simulators with authentic callsigns from actual contest databases, making your training as realistic as on-air operation.

What contests can I practice with Head Copy?

Head Copy includes training modes for CWops CWT, ARRL contests, CQ WPX,SKCC events, NS Sprint, and POTA activations with authentic exchanges and timing.

Is Head Copy suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Head Copy includes an interactive Morse code chart and LICW beginner training that's perfect for learning your first characters, plus advanced features as you progress.

Do I need an internet connection?

Head Copy works offline for most training modes. Internet is only required for downloading contest calendar updates and accessing the latest club member databases.

What platforms does Head Copy support?

Head Copy is currently available for iOS devices including iPhone and iPad. CarPlay support is coming soon for practicing during commutes.
