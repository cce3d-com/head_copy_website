🎯 My Mission

I created Head Copy because I believe Morse Code is more than just dots and dashes – it's a beautiful, efficient form of communication that deserves to thrive in the modern world. As both a software developer and amateur radio operator, I saw an opportunity to bridge traditional CW practices with cutting-edge technology.

❤️ This is a solo project – every feature, every line of code, and every design decision comes from my passion for both amateur radio and creating exceptional user experiences. Your support through subscriptions directly enables me to continue improving and expanding Head Copy.

Unlike other CW training apps that focus solely on character recognition, Head Copy emphasizes real-world application. I wanted to create something that would actually prepare operators for on-air contacts, contests, and the authentic amateur radio experience.

💻 Development Philosophy

As a software developer with over a decade of experience, I bring professional standards and user-centered design to amateur radio applications. Head Copy isn't just a side project – it's built with the same care and attention to detail I'd put into any commercial application.

Core Principles

  • User Experience First: Every feature is designed with the end user in mind
  • Authentic Practice: Training should reflect real on-air conditions and procedures
  • Progressive Learning: Support operators from beginner to expert levels
  • Community Focused: Built by and for the amateur radio community
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular updates based on user feedback

Technical Approach

Head Copy is built using modern iOS development practices with SwiftUI, leveraging advanced audio synthesis, real-time contest data integration, and carefully crafted user interface design. The app's QSO simulator represents thousands of hours of research into authentic amateur radio procedures and databases.

🌟 Why CW Training Matters

In an age of digital communication, Morse Code remains relevant and valuable for several reasons:

  • Emergency Communications: CW cuts through noise when other modes fail
  • Spectrum Efficiency: Narrowband CW maximizes our limited frequency allocations
  • Mental Discipline: Learning CW develops focus, memory, and cognitive skills
  • Historical Preservation: We're the guardians of this elegant communication art
  • Universal Language: CW transcends language barriers in international contacts
  • Technical Excellence: Operating CW well requires skill and precision

My Goal: Head Copy aims to make CW training enjoyable, effective, and accessible to operators worldwide. Every feature is designed to build real skills that transfer directly to on-air operation.

Your feedback and support make these improvements possible. As a solo developer, every subscription directly contributes to making Head Copy better for the entire amateur radio community.
